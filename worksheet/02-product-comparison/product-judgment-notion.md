## S5.4 Moat Phân Tích

### Moat mạnh nhất của Notion

   - Họ:
     - build company wiki
     - SOP

Nếu AI-native interface mới xuất hiện:
- Notion có thể mất abstraction advantage.

Lens 2 — Niche Down (Notion)
5 Niche Questions:

Q1 — Use cases horizontal không giải được? PASS — Notion excels in async collaboration and internal wiki creation, which horizontal AI tools like ChatGPT struggle to replicate effectively due to lack of deep integration and context retention.
Q2 — Proprietary data? PARTIAL — Notion leverages user-specific and domain-specific data (e.g., team projects, personal notes), but much of its data is user-generated and not inherently proprietary.
Q3 — Proprietary functionality? PASS — Notion's block-based system and deep integrations with other tools (e.g., databases, calendars) are difficult for horizontal AI to replicate.
Q4 — Better interaction model? PASS — Notion provides a structured and visual interface, which is more intuitive than the linear chat-based interaction of horizontal AI.
Q5 — Deep integration / accuracy / citation? PARTIAL — While Notion integrates well with workflows, it lacks advanced citation and accuracy mechanisms compared to specialized tools.
10x Bar: Notion meets the 10x bar in async collaboration and knowledge organization, offering significantly better structure and integration than horizontal AI tools.

Absorption Test: CÓ — timeframe 6-12 months. Notion's niche is defensible in the short term but may face challenges as horizontal AI evolves.

Jasper Comparison Risk: LOW — Notion's niche is well-defined and distinct from generic AI tools like Jasper.

Niche Verdict: STRONG

Strongest signal: Async collaboration and internal wiki capabilities.
Weakest signal: Limited proprietary data.
Biggest threat: Horizontal AI improving integration and context retention.
